# Crypto Basics

## Bitcoin (BTC)
- First and largest cryptocurrency by market cap
- Created in 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto
- Max supply: 21 million coins
- Current use case: Store of value, digital gold

## Key Metrics
- **Market Cap**: Total value of all coins in circulation
- **24h Volume**: Trading volume in last 24 hours
- **Hash Rate**: Computational power securing the network
- **Difficulty**: Network adjustment to block creation time

## Sentiment Indicators
- Social media trends (Twitter, Reddit)
- News coverage
- Institutional adoption
- Regulatory news
- Technical analysis patterns
